Kale & Pear Green Smoothie

  • Time: 11 Minutes
  • Serves: 4.75 cups
Ingredients Quantity
Water 1/2 Cup
Green Grapes 1 Cup
Orange 1 Medium, Peeled, Quartered
Barlette Pear 1/2, Cored
Banana 1 Small, Peeled
Kale 1 Cup
Ice Cubes 2 Cups
Directions:
Step 1: Place ingredients in the blender in the order that's listed and place the lid securely in place.
Step 2: Use the lowest speed setting and quickly increase to the highest speed.
Step 3:Blend for 45 seconds or until desired consistency is reached, using the tamper to press ingredients toward the blades.

Glazed Pecans

  • Time: 10 Minutes
  • Serves: 1 Cup
Ingredients Quantity
Whole Pecans 1 Cup
Pure Maple Syrup 1/4 Cup
Kosher Salt 2 Pinches
Directions:
Step 1: Over medium heat add maple syrup and pecans to a non-stick skillet. When you notice that the maple starts to bubble reduce the heat to low and simmer for 6 to 8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Step 2: During the last minute the maple syrup is mostly evaporated, stir until there is not more liquid and the nuts clump together (be careful you don't burn the nuts during this stage). Remove from the heat.
Step 3: Transfer pecans parchment paper in a single layer. Sprinkle with salt and cool to room temperature. Store in a covered container.
